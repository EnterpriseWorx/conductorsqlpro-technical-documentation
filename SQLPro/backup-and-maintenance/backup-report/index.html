Introduction

To get started select the backup and maintenance Icon and choose Backup Report:

Here you can drill down in period (selected month)/ groups and instances for looking at your backups.

Data is monitored on an instance level and database level. With the graph you will easily be able to see if there are any differences over time.

You can see the details with messages of what caused the errors/failures over the selected time period by simply selecting the view details icon.
